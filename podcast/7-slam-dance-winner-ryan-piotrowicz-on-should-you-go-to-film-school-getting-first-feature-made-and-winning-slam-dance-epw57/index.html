#9 — Brian McCarthy (Billions, Orange is the New Black, High Fidelity)

July 6, 2021

Listen on Apple Podcasts

New York City actor Brian McCarthy (Billions, Orange is the New Black, High Fidelity, Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt, StartUp, Law and Order SVU, The Americans) joins Alex to talk about their early days of improv at The Pit and Upright Citizens Brigade, life as an actor in the city, performance anxiety, booking gigs, and why you should get an agent.
